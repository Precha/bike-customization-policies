Orbea takes the middle ground: explicitly states "no returns," but preserves a "manufacturing defect" exception.

個人簽名選項帶來的四個 Policy 新要點 Four Policy Implications of the Personal Signature Option

Trek、Canyon、Orbea 三品牌均無上管個人簽名功能，Giant 為業界首創，需自行建立政策語境。簽名規格：正體字 / 斜體字二選一，限 15 個文數字。 None of the three brands — Trek, Canyon, or Orbea — offer a top-tube personal signature feature. Giant is pioneering this in the industry and must build its own policy framework. Signature spec: regular or italic font, max 15 alphanumeric characters.

① IP 保護：消費者須保證簽名文字不侵犯第三方商標或著作權（仿 Canyon T&C §14.3）
② 取消節點提前：字體確認、簽名送出後即進入施作程序（不可逆），Cancellation Policy 需明定「簽名確認後不可取消或修改」
③ 個資處理：簽名含個人姓名等識別資訊，Privacy Policy 須說明此欄位的收集目的與保存方式
④ Disclaimer 具體化：正體 / 斜體預覽字形與刻印 / 塗裝實際效果可能有差異（筆劃粗細、間距），需在 Configurator Disclaimer 明示 ① IP Protection: Consumer must warrant that signature text does not infringe any third-party trademark or copyright (modeled on Canyon T&C §14.3)
